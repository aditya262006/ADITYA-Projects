How to Play

Your objective is to repeat the sequence of flashing tiles.

  • The game starts at the Easy level.
  • A sequence of tiles will flash. Watch carefully!
  • Click the tiles in the exact same order they flashed.
  • With each correct sequence, you advance to the next level.
  • Lives are reduced for incorrect guesses. The game ends when you run out of lives.
  • On "Hard" and "Ultra Hard" levels, you get extra chances per mistake before losing a life.
