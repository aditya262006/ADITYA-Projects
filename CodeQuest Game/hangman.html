How to Play

Your goal is to guess the hidden word one letter at a time. For each incorrect guess, a part of the hangman is drawn.

  • You have 9 chances to guess the word before the hangman is complete and you lose.
  • The number of starting "wrong" parts drawn depends on the difficulty level.
  • Use the hint button for a free correct letter, but it will be a penalty on the hangman drawing. Hints are only available on Hard and Ultra Hard difficulties.
